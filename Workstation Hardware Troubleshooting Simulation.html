Workstation Hardware Troubleshooting

Review each issue and identify the most likely component involved based on the observed symptoms.

Issue 1
• System powers on • Fans spin • No display • Repeating beep codes during startup
Issue 2
• Power button pressed • No lights • No fans • No sound
Issue 3
• System powers on • Fans spin • No beep codes • Black screen
Issue 4
• System boots normally • Shuts down unexpectedly under load • Excessive heat observed • Loud fan noise • Issue began after performance settings were changed
Issue 5
• System powers on normally • Random restarts or blue screens • Stable at idle, crashes during heavy use • Issue began after BIOS/UEFI settings were changed
Issue 6
• System powers on • Fans spin • No display output • Monitor and cable confirmed working • Issue began after hardware or BIOS changes
